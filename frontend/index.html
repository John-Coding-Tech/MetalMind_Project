Risk > Price

        
The core principle behind every MetalMind recommendation.

      

      

        

          Cheap does not mean good. MetalMind never
          recommends the cheapest supplier blindly.
        

        
How it works

        
    
          
  • Risk score first — scored on certifications, contact info, reviews, domain quality, and pricing signals.
    • 
          
  • Value formula — value = price_score × (1 − 0.6 × risk). Higher risk directly reduces value.
    • 
          
  • AI cross-check — after ranking, AI audits the winner and flags anything the rules missed. Trust signal: Safe / Warning / Risk.
    • 
        

        

          💡
          Rule of thumb: If the price looks too good to be true, the risk score usually confirms it.
