# Product

AI system to find trusted ACP(Aluminium Composite Panel) suppliers and recommend the best value option.

## Goal

- Find ACP(Aluminium Composite Panel) suppliers in India and China
- Avoid risky or unreliable suppliers
- Estimate supplier prices from online data
- Recommend the best VALUE supplier (not just cheapest)

## Core Idea

This is NOT a price comparison tool.

This system prioritizes:
- Trust
- Risk
- Reliability

Price is secondary.

## Output

The system should return:

- Top 10 suppliers
- Recommended supplier
- Estimated price
- Risk level (Low / Medium / High)
- Reason for recommendation
